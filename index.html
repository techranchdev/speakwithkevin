What the book holds

To specification

Every fastener is measured before it is listed: thread pitch, shank, head, coating, proof load. If the caliper and the catalog disagree, the catalog is wrong and we reprint it.

Tabulated

One datasheet per SKU — torque tables, shear ratings, and the drawing with every dimension on it. Print it, laminate it, argue with it at the bench. The sheet wins.

In stock, in truth

The count on the page is the count on the shelf, recounted nightly. When it says six hundred, there are six hundred — and when it says none, we name the day more arrive.

Photographed like a drawing

Every product photograph runs through the steel duotone — desaturated, washed in the accent, printed like a screen print. The part reads as engineering, not marketing, which is what it is.
