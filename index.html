/ 09 Questions people ask

Fair enough — a few answers.

Is this actually you, or a team?+

It's me. Every reply, in my voice — because it is my voice. If you wouldn't want to talk to an assistant posing as me, good. Neither would I.

What does it cost?+

Breakthrough coaching is by conversation — ask in your first voice note and I'll tell you what fits. Venture Outfitter membership is $99/mo, or $79/mo for founding members. Strategic Advisory is scoped to the engagement.

How fast do you reply?+

Almost always within a few hours, always within 12. The rule is "when I have something useful to say," not "fast enough to look responsive."

Can we ever hop on a Zoom?+

Breakthrough is voice by design. A 60-minute call usually compresses to four 90-second notes — without you canceling your afternoon. For advisory and speaking, we'll find the right format.

Breakthrough vs. Venture Outfitter?+

Breakthrough is one-to-one with me — the deepest, most personal work. Venture Outfitter is the community of practice: a cohort of entrepreneurs, the coaching team, curriculum, and a private community. Many people do both.

What is Carbon Voice?+

A voice-messaging app built for exactly this kind of conversation — threaded, transcribed, searchable. Free for you. Install it, find me, press and hold.
